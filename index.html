ğŸ”½ Product

We believe that increase in level of understanding can be done through increased quality and comprehensibility of explanations.

To achieve this, we need to fundamentaly re-think and remedy limitations of tools we primarily use to communicate and explain knowledge today.

Understand Lab aims to address these challenges by creating Understand App that:

  1. Increases efficiency of creating explanation by shortening ideation and lead time through smart explanation builder and pre-defined templates
  2. Simplifies and increases quality of visual presentation through automatic designer
  3. Ensures consistency and re-usability of facts across explanations through defined common elements
  4. Adapt explanation form and content to consumers unique preferences through organizing elements in graph structures
